Basic Rules ↓

  • VISA Entry into the country is carried out on a visa. It can be issued directly at the airport
  • Documents At home, before departure, you must collect all the documents: a foreign passport with a valid visa, voucher, air ticket, if necessary, a notarized consent to the departure of the child
  • Medical insurance Medical insurance is also a very important document. It can be issued both in advance and at the place of stay.
  • First aid kit Tt is better to collect a first-aid kit in advance, adding the most necessary medicines, sunscreens and ointments to it
  • Import prohibited You can’t bring into the country with you: tobacco products, alcohol, money more than $ 1000, weapons, films, drugs
  • Arrival Upon arrival at the airport, you need to go through transport control, undergo a PCR test and collect your luggage. Upon arrival at the hotel, fill in the registration card and wait for check-in
  • Transport The only convenient form of transport for tourists in Bali is a taxi. We recommend using the Blue Bird Taxi or other metered taxis. Starting price on the counter - 7000 rupees
  • General rules Keep your belongings in the hotel safe. Rent a bike only if you are confident. Drive your bike only when sober. Always carry your driver's license with you. Better use cash. Change money only in official exchangers. Read about what reverse currents (rip currents) are and how to behave if you are caught in such a current. If you want to learn how to surf, don't do it yourself. In no case do not buy drugs from anyone and do not try if they offer you for free. For the possession and distribution of drugs in Indonesia, punishment is provided up to the death penalty!
  • Relationship with wildlife Bali has laws that protect the animal and plant world. It is forbidden to cut down trees, harm them (especially in protected areas), trample lawns, burn grass, make fires in dangerous areas. It is also forbidden to feed animals without the supervision of responsible people. Penalties for these violations can range from fines to prison
  • Climate The climate is tropical. Throughout the year it is hot and quite humid, the air temperature during the day fluctuates between 30-35 degrees, in the evening it is a little cooler. Humidity from 75% to 100% Therefore, if you have diseases that are provoked by high humidity or heat, then it is better to refrain from traveling
  • Language The state language is Indonesian, but a lot of people understand English, so we advise you to learn common phrases so that you can understand what they want from you and make it easier for you to find a common language with the local population. There are also a lot of Russian-speaking people in Bali, so if you don’t understand something, then you can resort to the help of other people
  • Departure from the country The day before departure, collect all your belongings, check if you have goods prohibited for export: Liquids (toothpaste, water, creams, perfumes, etc.) in containers of more than 100 ml are prohibited for export in hand luggage, it is also prohibited export some fruits (durian) and animals. You also need to pay for everything that you used on vacation
